How to create a Secret Message Link on VeilMsg

step 1

Generate your Secret Message Link

Enter your name into the above input box and click on ‘Get Your Link’. VeilMsg will create your guest account and a secret message link in less than a minute.

step 2

Share it on Twitter / WhatsApp / etc

Click the share icon on the home page to share your Secret Message Link on social media platforms like Twitter, WhatsApp, and more.

step 3

Wait for Secret Messages in your Inbox

Once you've shared your secret message link with your friends, watch for their quirky questions and confessions to show up in your inbox.

How to share your Secret Message Link on Twitter ?

Once you have created your secret message link, just click on the Twitter icon on your home page, it will automatically share your secret message link with your Twitter account.

How to share your Secret Message Link on WhatsApp ?

After creating a secret message link, click the share icon on the home page and then on WhatsApp to share with your friends through personal messages or via WhatsApp status.

What is a Secret Message Link ?

A secret message link is a link that allows you to receive anonymous messages on social media. Sharing the link allows your friends, followers to connect with you without revealing their identity.

How to see your Secret Message Link ?

If you already have a veil account, you can see the secret message link in the home page itself, else create a guest account. You can now copy or share that secret message link to whatsapp, facebook, twitter by clicking the share icon .

Is VeilMsg free ?

Yes, VeilMsg is free for everyone forever. It is open source. Join now as a guest or create a standard veil account and have fun.
