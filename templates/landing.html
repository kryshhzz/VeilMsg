What is a secret message link ?

VeilMsg allows you to share a unique URL called secret message link with your friends that they can use to send you anonymous questions or confessions.

This means that you can receive honest feedback and insights from your friends without knowing who sent them. You can use secret message links to have fun answering anonymous questions.
